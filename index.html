About Us

Late Popatbhai Premjibhai Maniya
(P.P. Maniya) Education & Medical Trust

A non-profit charitable organisation established with the objective of serving society through educational, medical, and social welfare activities. The Trust is dedicated to the upliftment of economically weaker and underprivileged sections of society — without discrimination of caste, religion, gender, or community.

Our Commitment

Operating with full legal compliance and transparency across Gujarat and India since 2019, the Trust continuously works towards educational growth, healthcare support, and social welfare with a commitment to humanitarian values.

✓ No caste discrimination
✓ All communities welcomed
✓ Gender equality upheld
✓ Serving all of India

Objectives of the Trust

Guided by a commitment to uplift every section of society, our trust pursues a broad mandate — from classrooms to clinics and open fields.

Promote Education

Extend quality education access to economically weaker sections of society.

Medical Assistance

Offer healthcare support and medical aid to those who cannot afford it.

Scholarships & Aid

Support students through scholarships, tuition aid, and educational grants.

Social Welfare Programs

Organize awareness camps and welfare drives benefiting underserved communities.

Yoga, Sports & Growth

Encourage yoga, sports, meditation, and holistic personality development.

Vocational Training

Provide skill development and vocational training for sustainable livelihoods.

Welfare of the Needy

Work actively for the welfare of poor and marginalized individuals across Gujarat.

Community Development

Foster sustainable community growth through inclusive welfare programs and outreach.

Environmental Awareness

Promote environmental conservation and sustainable living across communities.
